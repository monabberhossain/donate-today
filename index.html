Our Donate Plan

Each disaster is different, but we adapt our resources to the needs of the people we serve.
All day, every day, wherever someone needs us

Food For All Family

After a disaster, we works with community partners to provide hot meals, snacks and pure water served as shelters in affected neighborhoods.

Overnight Shelter Stays

We open shelters to make sure people have a safe place to stay, a hot meal and access to other support from trained volunteers.

First Aid Services

Our health volunteers travel to disaster sites to help people cope. Health workers can provide first aid treatment for injuries, monitor the well-being of people.

Humanitarian Relief

We work to equip health professionals in resource-poor communities to meet the challenges of diagnosing and caring for people in need. We provide services for Food, cloth, health, home and education.

Disaster Relief

Every day, we prepare the most vulnerable communities worldwide for more frequent, more destructive emergencies. And when disasters strike, Direct Relief responds fast, effectively, and efficiently to get medical resources where they need to be to save lives.
